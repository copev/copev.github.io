Sukiru

Inspired and influenced by the work of Japanese designer Ikko Tanaka, this project was a collaborative student work for the fictional Sukiru brand, a premium Japanese whiskey entering the market. The selection of a whiskey brand for this design pitch was also influenced by the desire to bridge the history of the drink's Scottish roots to an international style.

Relying heavily on cultural relationships for the brand identity inspiration drawn from Tanaka's process, we implemented a bold and expansive color palette with streamlined forms to create a unique, yet flexible system.
